Meet our service-minded team

Our team members are a part of the Office of International Services (OIS) at Indiana University (IU), so they understand what you need sunapsis to do. They’re also eager to help. Write to us at sunapsup@iu.edu.

Business Operations & Client Experience

Joe Compion

Director of Business Operations & Client Experience

A strategic leader dedicated to advancing the sunapsis mission, Joe brings a unique blend of technical expertise and operational discipline to the team. A proud Hoosier, he earned his bachelor’s degree in Informatics with a business cognate from Indiana University. His commitment to excellence was forged during his service with the U.S. Army's elite 101st Airborne Division, which included combat deployment to Kandahar, Afghanistan (Operation Enduring Freedom). When Joe steps away from work you can find him enjoying the simpler things: video games, music, football, and the relentless pursuit of the perfect taco.

Licensing and Client Engagement Team

Marie O'Connell

Licensing and Events Manager

Originally from Limerick Ireland, Marie came to Indiana in 1996 as an Au Pair for a year. She has worked at Indiana University since July 2008. Marie joined the Office of International Services in September 2015 as Executive Administrative Assistant for the Associate Vice President for International Services. Marie's role expanded over the years and she became part of the sunapsis team in 2019. She continues to expand her role with sunapsis. In her free time Marie enjoys boating, reading, traveling and knitting. She even knits some of her own sweaters.

Client Experience Team

Kathy Abell

Senior Client Solutions Engineer & Documentation Specialist

A native of Monroe County, Indiana, Kathy is an IU Bloomington alumna earning a BA in Economics and minors in Business and Psychology.  After working for IU for several years, she contracted as an IT professional in Kuwait and Moscow Russia.  While overseas, she, and her husband traveled extensively to several countries including Greece, Jordan, Turkey and Italy.  Kathy is happy to be home and enjoys gardening, reading and spending time with family and friends.

Amy Corwin

Client Solutions Engineer

Amy is a seasoned professional in international education with a deep understanding of the unique needs of international students, scholars and employees. She spent 13 years as an independent Sunapsis consultant, helping universities streamline their international business processes with this robust software. In August 2025, she brought this expertise in-house, joining the Sunapsis team directly. Originally from Oklahoma, she traded the scorching heat for the scenic beauty and snowy winters of Vermont. A true global citizen, she has a passion for travel and spent over seven years living abroad, most recently in Stockholm, Sweden, and speaks Swedish and rusty French. When not at work, you can find her indulging in her love for bread baking and painting, or simply enjoying time with her beloved dogs.
