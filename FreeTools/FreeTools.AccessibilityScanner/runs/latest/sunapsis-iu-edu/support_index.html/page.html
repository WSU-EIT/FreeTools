Support

Looking for help with sunapsis?

All of our support services are managed through our official sunapsis Support site. We encourage all sunapsis users to visit the site and create an account to get access to our comprehensive Knowledge Base, which includes:
  • Thousands of Articles: From user guides and tutorials to in-depth troubleshooting steps.
  • Bug Reports: A detailed list of known bugs with their current status and available workarounds.
  • Feature Requests: See what enhancements are being considered and track their development.
  • Version Updates & Release Notes: Download update packages for your sunapsis environment and review the detailed release notes.
This is your one-stop shop for everything you need to succeed with sunapsis.
Go to the support site

How to create a support ticket

If you can't find an answer in the Knowledge Base, our team is ready to help. There are two ways to open a support ticket:
  1. On the Support Site: Log in to the portal and submit a ticket directly. This is the best way to track your request's status and see all communication.
  2. Via Email: Send a message to sunapsup@iu.edu. This will automatically create a ticket in our support system, and a team member will contact you.
