Mobile App

Welcome to the first mobile app from an international case management system

It’s time to make forms device-friendly. It’s time to communicate with students and scholars with more than just email. It’s time to use a portal that matches your institution’s branding.

The sunapsis mobile app provides all of these things. Students and scholars can fill out forms through native iOS and Android apps. You can send push notifications to students (because let’s be real: students don’t read email anymore, do they?). You can match the app (and the 4.X version of the online services) to your institution’s branding.

Note: the mobile app is for the online services for students, scholars, and departments. It is not for administrative sunapsis users.

Help from the sunapsis team

Maintaining an app in the iOS and Google Play stores can be tricky. That’s where we come in. We’ll add your institution to the sunapsis app and maintain it in both stores so you don’t have to focus on Apple or Google app stores.
