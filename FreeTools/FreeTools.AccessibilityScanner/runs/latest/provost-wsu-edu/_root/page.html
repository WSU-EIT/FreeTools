    Washington State University Office of the Provost

    “As the chief academic officer for Washington State University, I have the distinct privilege to work with senior leadership, deans, faculty, and staff to shape the academic enterprise at this great institution. The system-wide strategic plan provides a clear and compelling blueprint for the future. As Washington’s land-grant research university since 1890, we have a deep, rich foundation on which to build and I am honored to have the opportunity to serve.”

    -T. Chris Riley-Tillman, Provost and Executive Vice President
