Student Financial Services Welcome to the Estimated Financial Aid Offer Portal

Initial Estimates - No Longer Available

At this time, new financial aid estimates for the 2023-2024 academic year are no longer being created.

  • If you need to access one of your previous 2023-2024 Financial Aid estimates, contact Student Financial Services (SFS) at 509-335-9711 or financialaid@wsu.edu for assistance.
  • If you submitted a FAFSA or a WASFA and don't have a 2023-2024 WSU Financial Aid Award yet, please check your myWSU account. Further information may still be needed to process your financial aid. If you need help, don't hesitate to contact Student Financial Services.


WSU employees/staff only: To view a student's previous 2023-2024 Financial Aid Estimate, you may login below.

Offer Portal

The Estimate Portal is for new WSU students and their families to view their online 2023-24 Financial Aid Estimate(s). If you are a current student, you can use the Financial Aid Estimator tool to assist you with estimating costs and aid options but an estimate through this system will not be available.

How to Review your Estimated Financial Aid Offer

To Sign In:

  • If you are a student, you will need a WSU Network ID and password to view your Estimate. Your Network ID is usually in the form of firstname.lastname. If you have not created a Network ID, go to Create Network ID to do so.
  • If you have forgotten your Network ID or password, please go to WSU Network ID Account Manager.
  • If you are a parent or a third party and want to view a student’s Estimate, click here for available options.
