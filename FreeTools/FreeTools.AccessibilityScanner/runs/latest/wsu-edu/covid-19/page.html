WSU COVID-19 Recovery Guidance

Thanks to the Cougar community for their efforts to fight the spread of COVID‑19 throughout the pandemic.

The health of our community as well as their loved ones remains paramount. Washington State University continues to track COVID-19 conditions across the state.

Have a Question?

  •  For questions related to course delivery, please email provost@wsu.edu.
  • For general questions related to student health please email cougarhealth@wsu.edu. 
  • For questions about documentation for all required student immunizations, please email  cougarhealth.immunizations@wsu.edu 
  • For questions related to the religious exemption process, please email vaccine.exemption@wsu.edu
  • For questions related to housing on the WSU Pullman campus, please email housing@wsu.edu.
  • For employee-related questions, please email hrs@wsu.edu.
