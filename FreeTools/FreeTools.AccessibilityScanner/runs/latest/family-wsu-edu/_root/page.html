    Hello WSU Families and Supporters!

    We are so glad you and your student have chosen WSU Pullman. This is a time of change for you all, and we’re honored to be your partner on this journey.

    Your role in your student’s life is changing, but you are still an essential part of their college experience. Whether you’re a parent, sibling, aunt, uncle, grandparent, neighbor, friend, or mentor, your support will be key to their success at WSU and beyond.

    There are several ways you can be involved, stay connected, and help your student navigate college life. On these pages, you’ll find information on everything you need to know as a family member and supporter of a Coug.

    Introducing the Coug Family Portal

    We can help you stay connected while letting your student grow and thrive on campus. 

    The Coug Family Portal is WSU Pullman’s new resource hub for families and supporters of current students. It offers timely campus updates, key deadlines, helpful articles, and personalized newsletters to help you stay connected and support your Coug throughout their college experience.

    Visit cougfamily.campusesp.com

    Note: The Coug Family Portal is for families and supporters of current students at WSU Pullman. Families of incoming students will continue to receive updates from Admissions and New Orientation Programs until just before their student’s first semester begins.

    Have something to share? 

    WSU departments can contact student.affairs@wsu.edu to inquire about contributing content to the Coug Family Portal.
