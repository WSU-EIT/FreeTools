Late Fee Petition

Students may request a review of late fees after their Tuition and Mandatory Fees are paid in full (late fees may remain unpaid). Submitting a petition does not guarantee that late fees will be removed. Please allow 4–8 weeks for processing. You will receive an email once the Committee has reviewed your petition.

If you have questions concerning your Grants, Scholarships, Waivers and Student Loans, please contact the Financial Aid Office:

Financial Aid
Phone: 509‑335‑9711 (option 1)
Email: financialaid@wsu.edu

After your Tuition and Mandatory Fees (minus late fees) are paid in full, you may submit a Late Fee Petition here: Petition a Late Fee
