Alumni Spotlight: Aaron and Jen Thorne

Alumni Aaron and Jen Thorne found more than degrees at WSU Vancouver. They built careers, discovered community and began their love story on campus. Today they continue to give back, supporting students and shaping the place that changed their lives.

Philanthropic couple matches gifts to WSU Vancouver's Cougar Food Pantry up to $10K.

GivingTuesday matching gift supports WSU Vancouver students facing food insecurity

WSU Vancouver is addressing student food insecurity with a $10,000 GivingTuesday matching gift for the Cougar Food Pantry, made possible by Mark Brubaker and Kristina Lockwood. The pantry is a vital resource as basic needs insecurity rises.
