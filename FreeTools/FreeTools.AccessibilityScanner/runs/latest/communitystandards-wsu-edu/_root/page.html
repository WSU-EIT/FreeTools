    Welcome to the Center for Community Standards


    Here for the WSU community.

    The Center for Community Standards is your partner in building a respectful, supportive, and inclusive campus. We help students, faculty, and staff navigate accountability with care—fostering well-being, integrity, and personal growth along the way. Whether you’re seeking guidance, support, or just have questions, we’re here to help you thrive at WSU and beyond.
