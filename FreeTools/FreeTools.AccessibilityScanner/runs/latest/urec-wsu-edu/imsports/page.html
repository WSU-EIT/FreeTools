Welcome!  Intramural sports are played “within the walls” of the university, amongst the community of WSU.  They provide a fun environment of playing sports you love with friends, in exchange for a small time commitment (1-2 hours per week).

You can sign up to play in any sport league or one-day event throughout the semester. You choose the day and time that would work best for you, and sign up yourself (or as a team) to play during that time each week.

We have 2 sessions each semester that offer a variety of sport leagues and events.  An Intramural Sports Pass costs just $35.00 for the semester, which allows you to play in as many leagues and events as you would like for both sessions. We also offer a Yearly pass for $65.00 which gives you year round Intramural access.

You can find Intramural passes on the Memberships Page. You must have a IM Pass Membership purchased to join a sport.

Scroll down to see all of our league sports and FREE event offerings!

Choosing the Right League

Elite League

In Elite leagues, you are someone looking to compete! This is the highest competition level that we offer, and typically only provided with our more popular sports.  Depending on the league, Elite provides more officials on games, up-to-date player and team statistics to view your progress throughout the season, and more. You will play in 4 weeks of pool play, and 2 weeks of playoffs for the potential to win an Intramural Championship T-Shirt.  All divisions within Elite are considered Open (see below). 

* Playoff games may be played on Sunday-Thursday, based on scheduling availability. 

A League

In A League, you are someone who has played the sport before and are looking to compete.  When Elite League is not offered, this is typically the highest level of competition.  You will play in 4 weeks of pool play, and 2 weeks of playoffs for the potential to win an Intramural Championship T-Shirt.

* Playoff games may be played on Sunday-Thursday, based on scheduling availability. 

B League

B League. You feel comfortable playing the sport, and you are looking just to play some friendly games. You will play 6 weeks of league games, with no playoffs (or T-Shirt) at the end.

League Divisions

What is the difference between all the divisions?

Co-ed. The Co-ed division is open to individuals who identify as male or female.  Each sport requires its own male-to-female ratio.  Please check each league to see how many men and women are needed to play a game.  Co-ed leagues often have modified rules compared to our other leagues.

Open. The open division is open to all people and is played by standard sport rules.

Women’s. The Women’s division is open to individuals who identify as women. All women's divisions compete in the A League. 

*All participants must also meet all other intramural eligibility requirements.

Ready to Register? Here's How:

Step 1: Choosing your Intramural Pass

Semester Pass:

This pass allows you to play in as many leagues and events as you would like for both sessions per semester.

  • Fall
    • Intramural Sports Fall 2025 Pass- $35.00
    • Intramural Sports Fall 2025 Pass- Spouse/Partner- $65.00
  • Spring
    • Intramural Sports Spring 2026 Pass- $35.00
    • Intramural Sports Spring 2026 Pass- Spouse/Partner- $65.00
  • Summer
    • Intramural Sports Summer 2025 Pass- $18.00

Yearly Pass: 

This pass gives you access to all intramural sports for the academic year. 

  • Intramural Sports Full Year Pass 2025-2026- $65.00
  • Intramural Sports 2025-2026 Full Year Pass- Spouse/Partner $100.00

*If you are a spouse/partner of a student/ Faculty/ or staff member who has a UREC membership, please purchase the spouse/partner pass.  

Purchasing your pass:

  • Follow the link to our membership page: Memberships Page 
  • In the search bar on the top right corner, type in "Intramural". This will show you a selection of Intramural memberships for the year.
  • Select your desired year OR semester membership.
  • Click "Sign Up".
  • Login using your university account.
  • Purchase your membership. 

Step 2: Register your team on DSE
  • Go to the Intramurals Page on DSE.
  • Click the blue "Log In" button at the top right corner of the sport list.
  • Login to your university account.
  • Select your desired sport(s). 
  • Under the season tab, select your desired sport session. 
    • This will show days, times, league types, and divisions available to register a team.
  • Select "View" on your desired league and time.
  • Click "Register your Team". 
  • Enter your team name & click "create".
  • Click "Add by Email" to invite teammates to register for your team. 
  • Reach out to urec@wsu.edu with any questions. 
Step 3: View your team schedule

Your team schedule will be published after the registration period ends. This may be on a Friday or Monday after the last day of registration. To see your schedule, go to:

  •  UREC Intramural Sports Page,
  • Log in using your university account.
  • Select the sport for your desired session.
  • Select view beside the league you registered for.
  • Your schedule will appear.

Instructions for Setting Up Facility Access

  1. Access the WSU DSE webpage
  2. Log in: Use "University Account" if you are a current WSU student, faculty, or staff. Use "Local Account" to login with a non-WSU email address associated with your UREC membership.
  3. Download the DSE Facility Access mobile app
  4. Once the app is downloaded, return to the Facility Access App webpage
  5. Click "Activate New Device" at bottom of website to get university code and activation code
  6. Open the app and enter the information/activation code to associate your phone with your DSE account.
