WSU Sport Clubs

The Sport Club Federation is comprised of student-run sport clubs that work together under the advisement and mentorship of University Recreation. Sport Clubs provide competitive programs for WSU students.

Each club elects its own officers, provides a representative to the Sport Club Federation Council, raises funds, arranges their competitions and activity schedules, and promotes their organizational activities.

To reach talk to UREC staff about joining or creating a club, please reach out to:

Assistant Director- Sports & Youth Programs

Login to your UREC Account>>

Current WSU students, faculty, staff, log in using your WSU Network ID (NID) and password.

Other individuals, please log in using the DSE Local Account you have created with your personal email address.  You will be able to review details of your purchases like memberships, lockers, and activities.

If you're having trouble accessing your account, or you need help:

Contact Us

Main Office | 509-335-UREC (8732) | urec@wsu.edu

Instructions for Setting Up Facility Access

  1. Access the WSU DSE webpage
  2. Log in: Use "University Account" if you are a current WSU student, faculty, or staff. Use "Local Account" to login with a non-WSU email address associated with your UREC membership.
  3. Download the DSE Facility Access mobile app
  4. Once the app is downloaded, return to the Facility Access App webpage
  5. Click "Activate New Device" at bottom of website to get university code and activation code
  6. Open the app and enter the information/activation code to associate your phone with your DSE account.
