Academic Advising

At Carson College of Business, our academic advising is a collaborative, student-centered relationship designed to foster your personal development and academic success. Your dedicated Carson College of Business academic advisor will support you every step of the way as you decide on, plan, and achieve your personal, educational, and career goals.

As you meet with your advisor every semester, they will:

  • Educate you on how to navigate the university and use resources, understand and plan your degree requirements, explore your options, and make decisions to support your goals.
  • Empower you to excel and take full advantage of the opportunities and network afforded to you as part of your Carson Coug experience.
