Frequency

This WSU Financial Aid handbook has several sections. Each section is presented with a table of contents and chapters with segments. Included are our processes, policies, procedures, helpful tools and references to the regulations that govern us. Each section of the WSU Financial Aid handbook has a yearly review by the subject matter experts and the Student Financial Services Leadership team. However, while each entire section of this handbook is reviewed once yearly, this is an ongoing document and the chapters therein are updated as necessary, thus it will vary based on new guidance, process changes, enhancements, etc.
