    Dining Events

    Tuesday, February 17, 2026 @ 4:30 PM

    Lunar New Year | Change of Taste

    Location: Northside Café
    Sponsor: Dining Services

    Celebrate the Lunar New Year with great food and festive decorations! Enjoy delicious dishes like Garlic Noodles, Hainanese Chicken and Rice, Dumplings, and more. This event is a joyful way to share tradition, flavor, and connection with friends and the Coug community.


    Wednesday, February 18, 2026 @ 4:30 PM

    City Food Tour: Destination Delicious - San Francisco

    Location: Southside Café
    Sponsor: Dining Services

    Take a culinary tour of San Francisco with a vibrant food-focused event celebrating the city’s iconic flavors. Enjoy Mission-style Burritos, Classic Rice-A-Roni, Pasta Pomodoro, a customizable Pho Bar, Clam Chowder in bread bowls, Beer-Battered Fish and Chips, Bay Shrimp Salad, and more local favorites. From comforting classics to diverse cultural staples, come explore the tastes that make San Francisco one of the world’s great food cities.

    Wednesday, February 25, 2026 @ 4:30 PM

    City Food Tour: Destination Delicious - Miami

    Location: Northside Café
    Sponsor: Dining Services

    Enjoy a culinary journey through the vibrant flavors of Miami! Guests will experience the city’s diverse tastes with favorites like Savory Arepas, Rich Cafecito, Cubano Fritas, Classic Cubano Sandwiches, Tangy Key Lime Pie, and comforting Ropa Vieja. This event is a delicious way to explore Miami’s culture and cuisine—one bite at a time.


    Thursday, February 26, 2026 @ 5:00 PM

    Skewered and Seasoned | Change of Taste

    Location: Hillside Café
    Sponsor: Dining Services

    Dig into tasty skewers like Chicken Teriyaki, Caprese, BBQ Pork & Apple, and Beef, with sides like Garlic Butter Potatoes, Asian Slaw, and more. Come eat, hang out, and enjoy!

    Friday, March 6, 2026 @ 7:30 AM

    Oreo Day

    Location: Northside Cafe
    Sponsor: Dining Services

    Celebrate the endlessly creative Oreo cookie with a sweet-focused food event in the bakery area. From Oreo ice cream and layered parfaits to cakes, cupcakes, and Oreo-studded churros, this event highlights the cookie’s incredible versatility in desserts. A playful and indulgent experience for anyone who believes Oreo is the ultimate sweet treat.


    Monday, March 9, 2026 @ 4:30 PM

    Meatball Mania

    Location: Northside Café
    Sponsor: Dining Services

    It’s raining meatballs! Celebrate National Meatball Day with a lineup of tasty favorites like Spaghetti and Meatballs, Meatball Pizza, Mushroom Meatballs, Meatball Albondigas, and more delicious surprises.

    Wednesday, March 11, 2026 @ 11:00 AM

    Jazzed Up Cajun

    Location: Hillside Café
    Sponsor: Dining Services

    Experience the soulful flavors of Cajun cuisine through a blend of Louisiana tradition and modern creativity. Explore the cultural roots of Cajun cooking while enjoying classics like Chicken Étouffée, Seafood Gumbo, and Cajun Popcorn Shrimp, along with fresh twists such as Blackened Tofu and Cajun Chicken Pasta Bake. A bold mix of history, spice, and modern flavor.
