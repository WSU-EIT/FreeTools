New! Department Email Dashboard

Pullman Department Email List

The Pullman Office of the Registrar maintains an email list of department contacts that are used for many of our processes, including:

  • SER: Special Enrollment Request approvals
  • RGU: Request Grade Update approvals
  • ETW: Employee Tuition Waiver approvals

Update department email contacts

A Department Coordinator, or a separate main department contact, can keep the list updated using the Department Email Dashboard. The main contact or department coordinator needs a free Smartsheet account to view and edit the assigned subjects and the appropriate emails. Instructions are on the dashboard. If you need assistance, email us.
