    Scott/Coman Hall, located on the north side of campus, is a co-ed community of nearly 300 students. Students living in Scott/Coman Hall can live and learn together in a safe community to develop leadership skills and refine academic focus.

    FAQ

    What common spaces are available?
    • There is one full kitchen, with microwave, oven, refrigerator and sink, on second floor in each building in the lounge.
    • Every floor has a microwave in the laundry room. 
    • The hall has a pool table, a Foosball table, two pianos, and two TV lounges.
    • Two 24 hour quiet study lounges located on the first floor of each hall that students can use to do homework or reserve for study groups.  
    Is there additional storage available?
    • There is one small room off the lobby in each building for storage.
    • Bikes can be stored under Regents inside a cage and on wall-mounted racks.
    What are furniture specifications?

    Beds

    • Bed frames are 85"L x 38"W.
    • Mattresses are 80"L x 36"W. Extra long sheets with deep pockets work best.
    • Bed risers can be used to gain additional storage.
    • Beds can be lofted or bunked with provided supplies. Kits must be stored in rooms when not in use.
      • Tool Free Bed User Guide (contact housing@wsu.edu for an accessible version.
      • How to Loft Your Bed Video
      • How to Bunk Your Bed Video
    • Maximum space under the bed is 56 ¾" when lofted and 31" when unlofted.

    Dressers

    • 42"H x 24"D with eight drawers.
    • Dressers are attached to one another.

    Desks

    • 42"W x 24"D with one small drawer
    • Includes a mobile pedestal with one drawer and one file drawer.
    • Includes desk chair.

    Drawers

    • Stacking drawers are each 20 1/2" H x 24" D x 25" W.
    What are room specifications?
    • Double rooms are 13'8"W x 14'7"L.
    • Single rooms are 14'7"W x 8'4"L.
    • Windows are 13'W x 3'1"H.
    • Closets are 88"H x 81"W x 2'D.
    • Each room has a mirror above a dresser unit between the closets. 
    • Four sets of outlets in each room.
    • Rooms are carpeted.
    • Microwaves are not permitted, but mini- or microfridges are.
    What laundry facilities are available?

    Every floor in Scott/Coman has a front-loading washer and dryer for residents' use. The fee to use them is included in your housing and dining bill.

    What is my mailing address?

    Pick up regular mail in Scott/Coman Hall. Pick up packages at the Northside Area Desk in the Northside Residence Hall lobby.

    What can I use to hang things on my walls?

    Please use blue painters tape for posters, and 3M Command strips to hang pictures.  If you use 3M Command strips, please do not remove the strips; our staff will remove them over the summer if the room needs to be painted.  Please do not use double-sided mounting products, permanent mounting products, blue mounting putty, tacks or push pins, masking tape, or duct tape.  Scott/Coman has several walls with wall carpeting; please use tacks and pins on these walls, never use 3M Command strips.
