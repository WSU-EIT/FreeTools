    Diverse & Energetic

    Gannon-Goldsworthy Hall, on the south side of campus, is an energetic, diverse and sociable residence hall. This hall has a culture of building connections and engaging in community for the students who live here.  Goldsworthy is home to the Wellness Living Learning Community, where resident focus on holistic self-care and growth.

    FAQ

    What common spaces are available?
    • Each floor has a study lounge and there are two large study lounges on the main floor.
    • Every floor has a microwave and there is a kitchen located in the basement.
    • The main lounge has a pool table, ping pong table, foosball table, big screen TV, and kitchen.
    What laundry facilities are available?

    Each floor has a washer and dryer. The cost is included in room and board.

    Is this hall accessible

    There are two wheelchair accessible rooms with private bathrooms and one hearing-impaired accessible room.

    Both buildings have elevators, making the floors accessible. The basement area, including the recreation room are also accessible.

    Is there additional storage available?

    There are bike racks located under Goldsworthy. 

    What are the furniture specifications?

    Beds

    • Mattresses are 80" L by 36" wide. Sheets with deep pockets fit best.
    • Bed frames are slightly longer than the mattress.
    • In Goldsworthy beds can be lofted or bunked. In Gannon, beds cannot be lofted or bunked.
      • Tool Free Bed User Guide (contact housing@wsu.edu for an accessible version.
      • How to Loft Your Bed Video
      • How to Bunk Your Bed Video
    • Space under the bed ranges from 8-32" when unlofted.
    • Bed risers can be used to gain storage space.

    Desks

    • 40" W x 52" H x 24" D,
      • Gannon: includes one shelf above and three drawers.
      • Goldsworthy: includes detached three drawer moveable cabinet.
    • Includes desk chair. 

    Dressers

    • Gannon: 27" W x 60 11/16" H x 24" D with two shelves in a cabinet and three drawers underneath.
    • Goldsworthy: three stacking drawers, 26 1/8" W x 10" H x 24" D each.

    Closets

    • 76" H x 45" W x 26" deep. A top shelf above the closet is 15" high.
    What are the room specifications?
    • There are 8 outlets in the room, 2 on each side.
    • Rooms are carpeted.
    • Windows are 10' W x 3.5' H.
    • Minifridges are permitted, but microwaves are not. 
    • Double rooms are 10'6" wide by 16'3¼" long.
    What is my mailing address?

    What can I use to hang things on my walls?

    Please use blue painters tape for posters, and 3M Command strips to hang pictures.  If you use 3M Command strips, please do not remove the strips; our staff will remove them over the summer if the room needs to be painted.  Please do not use double-sided mounting products, permanent mounting products, blue mounting putty, tacks or push pins, masking tape, or duct tape.
