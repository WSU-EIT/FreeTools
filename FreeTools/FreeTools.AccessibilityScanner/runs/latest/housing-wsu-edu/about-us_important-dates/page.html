    2025-26 Dates to Remember

    • August 9 & 10: Move-In Weekend
    • November 22-29: Residence Halls move to reduced operations for fall break
    • December 13: Residence halls move to reduced operations for winter break
    • January 7, 2026: Residence halls welcome new students for spring semester
    • February: Single Student Apartment applications open for 2026-27
    • March 14-21: Residence halls move to reduced operations for spring break
    • May 9: Residence halls close at 3 p.m.
