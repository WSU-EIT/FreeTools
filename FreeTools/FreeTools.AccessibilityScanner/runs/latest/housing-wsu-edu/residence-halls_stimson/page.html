    Home to Tradition

    Stimson has a rich history of leadership development and building service skills. This men's only residence hall is a place for residents to make lifelong friendships and find their niche at WSU. It has one of the highest retention of residents, who find community and help mentor other students.

    FAQ

    What common spaces are available?
    • There are two study lounges in the hall
    • There is one kitchen in the basement with a stove, microwave, refrigerator, and sink.
    • Stimson has a pool table, one weight room, ping pong table, and TV lounge with big screen TV.
    What laundry facilities are available?

    There is a laundry room with five front-loading washing machines and six dryers. The price is included in hall fees.

    Is this hall accessible?

    There are several wheelchair or hearing accessible rooms.

    Is there additional storage available?
    • There is a bike storage room in which 20-30 bikes can be stored.
    • Storage is available in a small basement room off the corridor leading the loading dock.
    What are the furniture specifications?

    Beds

    • Bed frames are 84" L by 40" W.
    • Mattresses are 80" L by 36" W. Sheets with deep pockets are advisable.
    • Beds cannot be lofted. 
    • Bed risers can be used to gain storage space. 

    Wardrobe/Dresser

    • Each wardrobe has a mirror.

    Desks

    • Each desk measures 47½"W by 23¾"D and includes four drawers.
    • Includes desk chair.
    What are room specifications?
    • Each room has an average of four outlets.
    • You may bring your own furniture, but WSU furniture should remain in the room.
    • Rooms are carpeted.
    • You may not bring a microwave
    • You may bring a mini-fridge

    What can I use to hang things on my walls?

    In Stimson please use the push pins we distribute during move-in.  Please do not use 3M mounting products, double-sided mounting products, permanent mounting products, blue mounting putty, masking tape, or duct tape.
