Lasting Connections

Rogers Hall is on the south side of campus and has a cohesive and engaging environment. This hall is popular with first year students who value spending time together to form close relationships. It is located minutes from Southside Café and Market and Stephenson Fitness Center.

FAQ

What common spaces are available?
  • There is a study lounge on each floor.
  • The active lounge includes a ping pong table, pool table, piano, and fireplace.
  • Every floor has its own full kitchen with oven, microwave, and sink.
What laundry facilities are available?

Every floor has a washer and dryer. There is also a laundry room on the first floor.

Is this hall accessible?

Several rooms are wheelchair or hearing accessible. 

Is there additional storage available?

There is one small storage room on the 12th floor.

Bikes can be stored in an uncovered outside bike rack.

What are the furniture specifications?

Beds

  • Mattresses are 80" L by 36" W. Sheets with deep pockets are advisable.
  • Bed frames are 85" L by 38" W.
  • Beds cannot be lofted or bunked.
  • Beds can have a maximum of 30" underneath.
  • Bed risers can be used to gain storage space.

Desks

  • Desk is 42" W by 24 " D
  • Includes keyboard drawer
  • Includes two file pedestals (16"W x 22¼"D X 24 1/8"H) that can fit under the desks
  • Includes desk chair. 

Dressers

  • The dressers are 3 separate stacking drawers, each measures 26 1/8"W x 24"D x 10"H

Shelves

  • There are two sets of shelves, 48" wide and 28" high

Closets

  • Closets are 44.6" W by 23" D by 80" H. 
  • Another foot of storage is available above the closet

Microfridge

  • Each room includes a microfridge that is 45" T by 18" W and about 20" D.
What are room specifications?
  • There is one full length mirror in each room
  • Each room has four double outlets
  • Each room has two computer hookups
  • You may not bring your own furniture
  • Rooms are carpeted
  • Windows are 8' W by 4" tall. A section 2'6" wide by 4' tall opens
  • Rooms are 10' wide by 16'8" long.

What can I use to hang things on my walls?

Please use blue painters tape for posters, and 3M Command strips to hang pictures.  If you use 3M Command strips, please do not remove the strips; our staff will remove them over the summer if the room needs to be painted.  Please do not use double-sided mounting products, permanent mounting products, blue mounting putty, tacks or push pins, masking tape, or duct tape.
