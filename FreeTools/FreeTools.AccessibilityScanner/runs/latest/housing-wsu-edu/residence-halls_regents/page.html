    Campus Involvement

    Regents is an all female hall located in a prime spot for women who are active and involved on campus. One of Regents Hall's primary objectives is to empower women through discussing topics such as women’s issues and accomplishments.

    FAQ

    What common spaces are available?
    • One kitchen with stove, microwave, refrigerator, and sink, on the second floor of the center building. 
    • There is a microwave in each floor laundry room.
    • There are two study lounges, one on each wing of the building.
    • There are also sunporches, formal lounge, and sky room.
    Is this hall accessible?

    Several rooms are wheelchair or hearing accessible. A public bathroom off the lobby is ADA-accessible.

    Is there additional storage available?
    • There is a moderate sized storage room in the basement near the loading dock. Staff can assist accessing it via the service elevator.
    • Bike storage is available under Regents inside a cage and on wall-mounted racks
    What are furniture specifications?
    • Bed frames are 85"L x 38"W.
    • Mattresses are 80"L x 36"W. Extra long sheets with deep pockets work best.
    • Bed risers can be used to gain storage space.
    • Beds can be lofted or bunked with the provided supplies. Kits must be stored in rooms when not in use.
      • Tool Free Bed User Guide (contact housing@wsu.edu for an accessible version.
      • How to Loft Your Bed Video
      • How to Bunk Your Bed Video
    • Maximum space under the bed is 56 ¾" when lofted and 31" when unlofted.

    Desks

    • 42"W x 24"D with one small drawer plus a mobile pedestal with one drawer and one file drawer.
    • Includes desk chair. 

    Dressers

    • Double rooms: 39.5"W x 23"D x 39.5"H. with eight drawers.
    • Single rooms:24.5"W x 23.5"D x 39"H. During the remodel of Regents
    • Two stackable drawers per bed.

    You can bring your own furniture but there is nowhere to store university furniture. There is limited storage for extra boxes, luggage, etc.

    What are room specifications?
    • Double rooms are on average 13'9" W x 15'6"L.
    • Single rooms are on average 7' W x 11'11" L.
    • Each room has a mirror above a dresser unit between the closets.
    • One small medicine cabinet per resident: 10"W x 18" H x 3"D
    • Four outlets total
    • Rooms are carpeted
    • Closets: 39.6"W x 69"H x 25"D. Storage units above closets and dressers are 39"W x 19.5"H x 25" D. There are four storage units in double rooms and two in single rooms.
    • Microwaves and portable air conditioners are not permitted.
    What laundry facilities are available?

    There is one laundry room for each living group (eight total) with two front-loading washers and four dryers. Laundry is included with housing and dining fees. Typically, residents of each floor sign up for a two hour laundry time per week with each floor having one day left open as a "free day" each week.

    What can I use to hang things on my walls?

    Please use blue painters tape for posters, and 3M Command strips to hang pictures.  If you use 3M Command strips, please do not remove the strips; our staff will remove them over the summer if the room needs to be painted.  Please do not use double-sided mounting products, permanent mounting products, blue mounting putty, tacks or push pins, masking tape, or duct tape.  Regents has several walls with wall carpeting; please use tacks and pins on these walls, never use 3M Command strips.
